## Idea A — "Iberian Brutalism Meets Technical Precision"

**Design Movement:** Portuguese Industrial Modernism — raw concrete textures fused with precision engineering aesthetics.

**Core Principles:**
1. Heavy typographic contrast: ultra-bold condensed display font against fine-weight body copy
2. Asymmetric column grids with deliberate negative space punched through sections
3. Monochromatic base (near-black, warm off-white) punctuated by a single electric amber accent
4. Technical credibility through data-dense trust strips and specification-style tables

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#1A1A18) backgrounds for authority, warm off-white (#F5F0E8) for body sections, electric amber (#E8A020) as the sole accent — evoking the Algarve sun without resorting to cliché blue skies.

**Layout Paradigm:** Offset grid — hero text bleeds left while image occupies a right-angled column. Sections alternate between full-bleed dark and warm-white panels. No centered hero.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal rule lines separating sections (like technical drawings)
- Monospaced numbers for statistics and service specs
- Bold condensed heading font (e.g. Barlow Condensed) paired with Lato for body

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal technical spec overlays. CTAs use a fill-animation on hover.

**Animation:** Sections slide in from left on scroll; numbers count up on entry; CTA buttons have a 160ms fill sweep.

**Typography System:** Barlow Condensed (700/800) for headings, Lato (400/500) for body, monospace for numbers/specs.

## Idea B — "Coastal Craft — Warm Mediterranean Professionalism"

**Design Movement:** Mediterranean Artisan — the warmth of Portuguese terracotta and whitewashed walls translated into a modern service brand.

**Core Principles:**
1. Warm terracotta and deep teal as primary palette — immediately evoking the Algarve without stock-photo clichés
2. Generous whitespace with organic section breaks (subtle wave or angled dividers)
3. Humanist typography — a serif display for authority, a rounded sans for approachability
4. Photography-forward hero with strong overlay gradient to ensure text legibility

**Color Philosophy:** Terracotta (#C4622D) for primary actions and headings, deep teal (#1B4F5A) for trust sections and footer, warm sand (#F7F0E3) as background — the palette of Algarve rooftops and ocean.

**Layout Paradigm:** Left-anchored hero with large image occupying 55% of viewport. Services in a 3-column asymmetric grid. FAQ in a two-column split. No full-center layouts.

**Signature Elements:**
- Subtle terracotta-tinted section dividers
- Rounded card corners with soft drop shadows
- Serif display font (Playfair Display) for H1/H2, Inter for body

**Interaction Philosophy:** Cards lift gently on hover (translateY -4px, shadow deepens). CTAs pulse subtly on load to draw attention.

**Animation:** Fade-up entrance for each section (opacity 0→1, translateY 20px→0, 400ms ease-out). Stagger 60ms between grid items.

**Typography System:** Playfair Display (700) for display headings, Inter (400/500/600) for body and UI, tabular numerals for stats.

## Idea C — "Technical Authority — Deep Navy & Precision White" ← CHOSEN

**Design Movement:** Engineering Precision — the visual language of premium HVAC and energy-systems brands: deep navy authority, crisp white space, and a warm gold accent that signals quality and the Algarve sun.

**Core Principles:**
1. Deep navy (#0D2137) as the dominant tone — communicates trust, expertise, and premium positioning
2. Warm gold (#C9922A) as the sole accent — conversion CTAs, section highlights, and certification badges
3. Off-white (#F8F6F2) for content sections — warmer than pure white, avoids clinical coldness
4. Structured asymmetry — hero splits into a text column and a full-height image panel; no centered layouts

**Color Philosophy:** Navy is the colour of engineering confidence and the Algarve night sky. Gold echoes the Algarve sun, Daikin's premium positioning, and the warmth of a well-heated home. Off-white provides breathing room without the sterility of pure white.

**Layout Paradigm:** Split-panel hero (text left, image right, full viewport height). Services in a 2+3 asymmetric grid. Process as a horizontal numbered timeline. FAQ in a single-column accordion with left-border accent. Footer in a 4-column grid.

**Signature Elements:**
- Gold left-border accents on blockquotes, testimonials, and FAQ items
- Thin horizontal rule dividers between major sections
- Section numbers in large muted navy numerals as background decoration

**Interaction Philosophy:** CTAs use a gold border sweep on hover. Cards elevate with a 2px shadow deepening. Nav items underline from left on hover.

**Animation:** Hero text fades in with a 30px upward drift (500ms ease-out). Section content animates in on scroll with 50ms stagger per item. Stats count up on entry.

**Typography System:** DM Serif Display (400) for H1/H2 — elegant authority without being stuffy. DM Sans (400/500/600) for body, labels, and UI. Tabular numerals for statistics and process steps.
